Position: Gardener / Maintenance Worker

We’re looking for hardworking and passionate individuals who love working outdoors and caring for plants. Join our growing team and help make every garden greener and more beautiful!

Basic Requirements

  • At least 18 years old
  • Physically fit and able to work under the sun
  • Basic knowledge of gardening or landscaping (training provided)
  • Good teamwork and communication skills
  • Honest, responsible, and punctual

Preferred Skills

  • Experience with plant care, trimming, or pruning
  • Knowledge of garden tools and safety procedures
  • Understanding of soil and fertilizers
  • Basic landscaping or design skills

Job Responsibilities

  • Maintain lawns, gardens, and outdoor areas
  • Plant, prune, and weed plants and shrubs
  • Assist with landscape design and setup
  • Inspect plants for pests and diseases
  • Ensure cleanliness and orderliness of the work area
