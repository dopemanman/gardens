Company Profile

GardenCare Co. is a student-founded garden maintenance business in the Philippines, focusing on affordable, reliable, and eco-friendly gardening solutions. We serve homes, schools, and small businesses that want to keep their green spaces healthy and beautiful.

Vision, Mission, Goals, and Objectives (VMGO)

Vision: To be the leading garden maintenance service that nurtures greener, cleaner, and healthier communities.

Mission: GardenCare Co. is committed to making home gardening easy and accessible. Through our passion for plants, we aim to inspire growth, wellness, and a deeper connection to nature.

Goals: To reach 500 homes and expand services to multiple communities.

  • Promote eco-friendly gardening practices
  • Support local plant growers and suppliers
  • Deliver reliable, high-quality service

Objectives: Maintain a 95% or higher customer satisfaction rate while growing sustainably.
